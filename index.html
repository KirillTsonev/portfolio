About me

My name is Kiril

I don't typically like to talk about myself, but here goes nothing. Born in Ukraine, raised between Bulgaria and Ukraine, 25 years old, an aspiring front-end software developer. The thing I like most about coding is making things work, and fixing things that are supposed to work, but don't. The whole process is like one big complex puzzle and I'm all for it. My strongest quality is my memory and I try to put it to good use: I speak 5 languages and am pretty good at chess. Hopefully I can put that memory of mine to also earn a living doing what I love.
